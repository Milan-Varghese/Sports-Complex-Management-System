About College:

AISAT KALAMASSERY

AISAT is a leading educational institution offering quality education in the field of engineering. The institution is spread over a picturesque area of 12 acres in a beautiful and serene atmosphere ideally suited for technical education. AISAT has a well-structured infrastructure of international standards to help the students to creatively apply scientific principles to design or develop structures and machines. AISAT fosters all round development of a student by incorporating major genres of education like reasoning, psychomotor and emotional learning to socialize students to become useful members of the society.
